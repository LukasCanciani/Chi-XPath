  • Luxurious suites. Queen Victoria?s largest suites are enjoyed by Queens Grill guests. All are sumptuously furnished with marble surfaces and high quality upholstery. Grills guests also enjoy exclusive access to the Grills Lounge, a private outdoor terrace and the renowned Queens Grill restaurant and courtyard. Queens Grill
  • Queens Grill butler. Your holiday will be perfect ? your butler will see to that. Every need will be discreetly anticipated and every detail seen to. Queens Grill
  • Queens Grill restaurant. The Queens Grill restaurant is the pinnacle of Cunard?s dining experience. Our chefs prepare the finest dishes with the freshest ingredients, which means that breakfast, lunch and dinner will all be something to savour. You can also enjoy the sun in the Grills courtyard, which offers exclusive al fresco dining from the same menu. Queens Grill
  • Grills Terrace. A space reserved exclusively for you, the Grills Terrace offers commanding views from the top from the ship. Staff will ensure your every comfort is seen to ? from cooling drinks to fresh fruit, soft towels and the perfect sunbed position. Queens Grill
  • The Princess Grill experience.
  • Luxurious suites. The combination of beautiful décor and comfort make Princess Grill suites the perfect sanctuary. Large suites with balconies, offer a retreat from the every day and ample space to relax and unwind. Princess Grill
  • Exceptional dining. Menus showcasing the very best ingredients combine with exceptional cooking, exclusive surroundings and impeccable service to make every meal in the Princess Grill restaurant and courtyard memorable. Princess Grill
  • Extra special touches. The famous Cunard Afternoon Tea is given a special touch when enjoyed in your exclusive Grills Lounge, and the additional Penhaligon?s toiletries found in your suite offer extra opportunities to pamper yourself. Princess Grill

  • The Britannia Club experience.
  • An amazing view. Britannia Club staterooms are spacious both on the inside and outside. Large windows and a light-filled balcony make al fresco breakfasts or a champagne sunset all the more appealing. Britannia Club
  • Britannia Club dining. You're welcome to dine whenever you choose between 6.30pm and 9pm in the dedicated Britannia Club Restaurant. The perfect setting for those who prefer a more intimate dining experience. Britannia Club
  • Complimentary room service. Whether you decide to treat yourself to breakfast in bed, or simply choose to dine in the comfort of your stateroom, 24-hour complimentary room service means that freshly-prepared food is always just a phone call away. Britannia Club
  • The Britannia experience.

  • Oceanview. Natural light and stunning views create a perfect oasis. An ever-changing seascape will captivate you, no matter where in the world you are. Britannia Oceanview
