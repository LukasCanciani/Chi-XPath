Accommodation

  • Princess Grill. Travel in true Cunard elegance.
Elegant luxury.
Your Princess Grill Suite is the perfect place to relax and unwind. Luxurious features such as a private balcony, separate seating area and a spacious bedroom make your Princess Grill Suite the ideal home from home. You also enjoy access to the Grills Lounge, Grills Terrace and renowned Princess Grill restaurant.

Why you?ll love it.

  • Princess Grill restaurant.

    Your intimate and exclusive Princess Grill restaurant serves innovative dishes and Cunard classics, prepared using the freshest ingredients and matched perfectly with fine wines from around the world. Alternatively, you could choose to dine in the exclusive Grills Courtyard ? situated right next to your restaurant.

  • Exclusive lounge access.

    Grills guests enjoy access to an exclusive lounge and terrace. Afternoon Tea can be enjoyed here, or perhaps a pre-dinner cocktail as the sun sets.

  • Concierge service.

    From bookings to dine in the on board specialty restaurants to personalised excursions ashore, your concierge will take care of every detail.

Services and amenities.

  • Bon Voyage sparkling wine and chocolates on arrival
  • Concierge service
  • Mingle with your companions in the Courtyard, Grills Lounge or Grills Terrace
  • Exclusive Afternoon Tea in the private Grills Lounge
  • Dine any time between 6.30pm and 9pm at your reserved table in the world-renowned Princess Grill restaurant
  • Alternatively dine in-suite course by course from the Princess Grill restaurant menu
  • Priority embarkation
  • King-size bed (converted to two single beds on request) with exclusive Sealy Cunarder Mattress, and choice of plush pillows from the Pillow Concierge Menu
  • Full bathroom with tiled floor, bath and shower with Penhaligons toiletries
  • Sitting area with elegant armchair and/or sofa
  • Private balcony with fully glazed exterior, teak deck, teak handrail on top of the balustrade and balcony furniture
  • 24-hour complimentary room service
  • Nightly turndown service with chocolate
  • Fresh flowers and daily fresh fruit
  • Space for your finest in the spacious walk-in wardrobe
  • Personalised stationery and atlas
  • Flat screen satellite TV with multi-language film/music channels
  • Direct-dial telephone
  • 220V British 3-Pin and 110V 2-Pin Sockets
  • Daily Programme and daily shipboard newspaper
  • Writing desk with drawers and chair
  • Soft velour robes and matching slippers, 100% cotton bed linens and towels
  • Other essentials include: refrigerator, key code safe, hairdryer
  • Air conditioning
  • Tea and coffee making facilities including illy coffee machine

Explore our other suites and staterooms.

    • Queens Grill.

      The largest suites on board Queen Elizabeth are reserved for Queens Grill guests. All are furnished in real wood and hand-made upholstery. Grills guests enjoy exclusive access to the Grills Lounge, outdoor terrace and the renowned Queens Grill restaurant and courtyard.

      Queens Grill

    • Britannia Club.

      With balconies in every Britannia Club stateroom you can enjoy stunning al fresco breakfasts, fresh sea air and natural daylight. Your balcony is the perfect private space to relax.

    • Britannia Balcony.

      The sea breeze and stunning view combine to make your balcony a wonderful place to while away the hours ? where better to enjoy a morning coffee and croissant?

      Natural light and stunning views create a perfect oasis. An ever-changing seascape will captivate you, no matter where you are in the world.
