Queen Victoria

Throughout her accommodation, bars and restaurants Queen Victoria exudes timeless splendour and elegance. This, combined with a sublimely relaxing atmosphere, creates the perfect holiday destination.

Refit for a Queen.
From 5 May to 4 June, Queen Victoria entered dry dock for a partial refit at the Fincantieri shipyard in Palermo, Sicily. This included an upgraded and increased choice of staterooms, new Britannia Club dining, a broader and more distinct bar offering, a transformed sun deck, and several other new features.
Discover more

Luxury down to the smallest detail.

From the ornately decorated boxes in the Royal Court Theatre through to the wood panelled library, Queen Victoria?s beauty lies within her details.

    • The Grills experience.

      Grills suites represent the very best of Cunard. Sumptuous furnishings, thoughtful service and space to relax create an atmosphere of indulgence and luxury. Around the ship, as a Grills guest, you will enjoy access to the exclusive Grills Lounge, Terrace and famed Grills restaurants.

      Discover luxury

    • Exceptional dining.

      Queen Victoria?s cuisine is as varied as it is delicious. The Grills restaurants offer exceptional dining for Grills guests and the Britannia and Britannia Club restaurants serve freshly prepared dishes for breakfast, lunch and dinner. For a small supplement try The Verandah for French specialties. Alternatively, for more relaxed dining with live entertainment, visit the Lido deck for al fresco dining - perfect for sailing around the Mediterranean.

      Discover exceptional dining

    • World-class entertainment.

      Queen Victoria?s Royal Court Theatre will leave you in awe, even before a performer steps on stage. The private boxes and traditional stage wouldn?t look out of place on London?s West End. Elsewhere, live music flows throughout the day and night in many venues across the ship.

      Discover entertainment

    • A historic connection.

      Cunard?s illustrious past comes to life throughout Queen Victoria. You?ll see echoes of it in her design, in the thousands of pieces of artwork and especially in the Maritime Quest exhibition where many original artifacts telling the QV story.

    • Fun and games.

      Traditional deck games are still very much a part of life on board, and you?ll have the opportunity to freely hone your skills. Sports enthusiasts can enjoy an energetic game of tennis on the courts on deck 11, or appreciate the traditional nautical games of shuffleboard and deck quoits. Inside the ship, pub games, quizzes, board games and puzzles are always taking place and participants are always welcome ? the dedicated card room is the perfect space to play.

      Discover the Games Deck

    • Promenade deck.

      Promenading has long been a cruising tradition. Queen Victoria?s promenade deck stretches right around the ship, offering stunning views, bracing sea air and teak steamer chairs to simply recline in, relax and unwind.

    • Elegant evenings.

      As the sun sets, Queen Victoria is abuzz with excited anticipation. Ball gowns and dinner jackets are donned in preparation for elegant balls, a night at the theatre in your own private box or signature cocktails and canapés in the Chart Room.

      Discover elegant venues.

    • Royal Spa.

      Relax and unwind in QV's luxurious pools, steam rooms or on a warm tile bed whilst taking in the sea view. Indulgent signature massages, hair styling and specialist treatments complete your pampering experience.

      Discover relaxation

    • Gain an insight.

      Cunard Insights attract famous faces from the arts, industry and beyond to entertain and enthral guests with lively presentations and discussions. Additionally, experts lead diverse workshops on topics from watercolour classes to fencing.

      Discover Cunard Insights.

    • Afternoon Tea.

      Indulge in this truly British tradition. White-gloved waiters serve decadent fresh scones, finger sandwiches and exceptional Twinings tea in the Queens Room. For a small supplement, enjoy a glass of exquisite Laurent-Perrier champagne alongside your afternoon tea.

      Discover Afternoon Tea

    • Library.

      Spread over two decks and linked by a wooden spiral staircase, the library offers an opportunity to borrow a bestseller or plan your next adventure. You?ll find magazines and periodicals here, as well as daily crosswords to exercise your brain.

      Discover the library
