Thomson Majesty serves up a master class in elegance. This ship is all about classically-styled bars and lounges made for after-dinner tipples, and a sumptuous spa and beauty salon with a menu of his ?n? hers treatments. You?ve got the pick of 5 restaurants, too, plus a great mix of live entertainment from our talented musicians and UK guest acts. All told, it?s a winning formula.

  • 11 Decks

DINING ? View all DINING

Le Bistro Restaurant ?

Our cosy à la carte eatery gives you the opportunity to spoil yourself rotten. Its calling card is a menu with a Gallic accent ? think lobster thermidor and crêpes suzette. Just the ticket for a romantic dinner for two.

Just so you know, a cover charge applies and you?ll need to make a reservation.

Four Seasons Restaurant ?

This elegant space is a more intimate alternative to the main dining room in the evening. It offers full waiter service and a wide menu choice.

ENTERTAINMENT ? View all ENTERTAINMENT

Jubilee Lounge ?

This is the focal point of the evening entertainment. Most nights, our talented team of singers and dancers take to the circular stage for choreographed performances. Other times, UK comedians and guest acts have been known to raise the roof. Late-night cabarets are held here, too.

Frame 52 Disco ?

There?s a roomy dance floor here and plenty of seating for relaxing or chatting with new friends. By day, Frame 52 is home to spa demos and fitness classes. By night, it transforms in to a disco.

FACILITIES ? View all FACILITIES

Sana Wellness Centre, Beauty Salon and Gym ?

The Sana Wellness Centre, Beauty Salon and Gym is the ultimate relaxation and pampering zone when you fancy spoiling yourself. It?s got 7 treatment rooms ? including a couples? massage room ? 2 saunas, and a beauty salon that lines up manicures, pedicures and hair stylists to get you at your head-turning best.

Main Pool ?

The main pool on the sun deck is surrounded by tables and chairs for relaxing and has plenty of family appeal.
