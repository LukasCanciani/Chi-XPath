Celebrity Millennium

The grand and luxurious Celebrity Millennium is the perfect base from which to explore worldwide destinations, from Alaska to Asia.

This ship is a firm favourite among our guests for a reason (well, many reasons). When you?re not out exploring new and exotic places, take yourself on a journey of a different kind: Celebrity Millennium offers a range of features and activities that celebrate the timeless appeal of cruising. From the relaxing and rejuvenating Canyon Ranch SpaClub and the inspirational Art Gallery to the new Rooftop Terrace showing open-air movies, you?ll be spoilt for choice.

When night falls, look no further than Cellar Masters, a cosy and intimate venue serving a range of beers and wines. The Cosmos Lounge is the perfect place to dance the night away, surrounded by stunning views.

Key facts

  • Occupancy: 2,138
  • Tonnage: 91,000
  • Inaugural date: Jun 17, 2000
  • Length: 965 ft
  • Cruise Speed: 24 kts
  • Draught: 26 ft
  • Beam: 105 ft

Onboard Gallery

The best of our Millennium Class ships, handpicked by us.

On your cruise, make sure you take in a show in our Main Theatre. Cutting-edge productions from fairy tales to comedies, stadium-style concerts to musical theatre. There?s something for everyone.
For big, bold flavours, try Tuscan Grille ? our speciality ristorante and enoteca. Regional Italian wines, succulent dry aged steaks, and outgoing service give Tuscan Grille its rustic charm.
Our indoor, adults-only Solarium is the perfect, tranquil place to unwind. Take a dip in our indoor thalassotherapy pool, relax in one of our hot tubs, or just watch the world go by.
Indulge in the largest and most comprehensive spa at sea, Canyon Ranch SpaClub. Or how about a visit to our Persian Garden? Take in the steam and relax on hot stone loungers.
Prepare to try new things when you dine at Qsine. From the globally inspired menu, to ordering your food with an iPad, to the pioneering presentation.
Our ice-topped Martini Bar is the hub of our fourth deck, and the perfect place for pre-dinner drinks. Choose your favourite from our menu of classic and contemporary martinis.
