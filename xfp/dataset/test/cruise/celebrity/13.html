Celebrity Xperience

One of three Celebrity Cruises ships sailing to the legendary Galapagos Islands, Celebrity Xperience is our mid-sized ship where absolutely nothing is middle-of-the-road. With a capacity of 48 guests, you?ll find it easy to unwind and get to know your fellow travellers.

Offering a choice of 7-night, 10-night, and 13-night cruises, you can choose how much of this mythical archipelago you?d like to explore on your Galapagos cruise.

From the comfort of our Sun Deck you can soak up the spectacular panoramas and spot rare wildlife ?why not spend the afternoon whale watching? When you?ve worked up an appetite, head to our outdoor dining spot, Al Fresco.

In our Lounge Bar, you can delve into a good book, watch a documentary, enjoy a refreshing drink, or take part in a briefing from one of our Galapagos National Park certified onboard naturalists.

Key facts

  • Occupancy: 48
  • Tonnage: 1,610
  • Inaugural date: March 2017
  • Length: 210 ft
  • Cruise Speed: 11 kts
  • Draught: 11 ft
  • Beam: 41 ft
