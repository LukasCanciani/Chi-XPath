Celebrity Xploration

One of three Celebrity Cruises ships sailing to the legendary Galapagos Islands, on Celebrity Xploration you can say ?hello? to the sense of community that can only be enjoyed on a small ship. With a capacity of sixteen guests, this charter-only ship is perfect for families or small groups.

The small size of the Celebrity Xploration allows us to take you deeper into this mythical archipelago than ever before. To further enhance your experience, you?ll be indulged with seamlessly intuitive service from our onboard crew. It?s a once in a lifetime holiday, in every way.

For the best views of your unique surroundings, head to the Sun Deck. You may even be treated to an afternoon of whale watching! Alternatively, relax and unwind in our indoor lounge. Settle down with a good book or broaden your knowledge by taking part in a briefing from one of our Galapagos National Park certified onboard naturalists.

Key facts

  • Occupancy: 16
  • Tonnage: 319.5
  • Inaugural date: March 2017
  • Length: 98.3 ft
  • Cruise Speed: 10 kts
  • Draught: N/A
  • Beam: 36.7 ft

Onboard Gallery

The best of Celebrity Xploration, handpicked by us.

Al Fresco: Open during the day and night. Great place to recount the day's activities and relax with friends. In addition, lunch, and on special occasions dinner, is served here.
Lounge: Its relaxing indoor atmosphere is perfect for reading, watching a documentary, or having a cocktail. In addition, this is the area where most Naturalist briefings take place.
One of the most popular areas onboard the ship, the Sun Deck is ideal for whale watching, stargazing, or relaxing in the hot tub.
